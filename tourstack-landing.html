Platform workflow

From discovery
to execution.

Four steps take a tour from announcement to a financier-ready decision, on a single rail.

  1. 01
    Step 01

    Ckrowd announces tour & artistes

    Admin creates a Tour Project on TourStack, including artiste roster, fee range, availability dates, and technical requirements.

  2. 02
    Step 02

    Promoter submits Expression of Interest

    Promoters browse available artistes and tours, then submit a formal EOI with their venue, date, budget, and funding model.

  3. 03
    Step 03

    Matching & review

    TourStack scores each EOI against artiste requirements. The Ckrowd team reviews flagged matches and makes informed decisions.

  4. 04
    Step 04

    Report & decision

    The system generates a business summary, estimated revenue, costs, and risk notes, structured for financiers. Admin approves, rejects, or requests revision.

Answers

Frequently asked questions

TourStack is a B2B platform for the live-entertainment ecosystem: promoters, artists and managers, venues, financiers, insurers, vendors and service providers. Each stakeholder onboards, gets verified, and works on a single trusted rail.

We structure a tour as a financial product and hold capital with Tier 1 banking partners in our operating markets. Funds release against milestone based smart escrow, so disbursements trigger only when verified conditions are met.

Promoters browse available artists and tours, then submit an EOI with venue, date, budget and funding model. TourStack scores each EOI against the artiste requirements and our team reviews flagged matches before a decision is made.

Working with our insurance partners, we use automated underwriting and a proprietary risk dataset to procure event cancellation and non appearance cover, making comprehensive tour insurance accessible continent wide.

Full financial infrastructure in Nigeria, South Africa, Kenya, Ghana, Rwanda and Uganda, with expanding compliance coverage across 18+ additional African territories.
